About Us

Welcome to Our Parking Management System

We offer a state-of-the-art Parking Management System designed to make parking seamless and efficient. Our system features advanced car plate recognition technology that automates the entry process, ensuring smooth operation and enhanced security.

How It Works

When a vehicle enters the parking area, a high-resolution camera captures an image of the car’s identification plate. This image is then securely uploaded to our server, and a timer is automatically activated to monitor the duration of the parking session.

Key Features

  • Automated car plate recognition
  • High-resolution image capture
  • Secure data storage
  • Real-time parking duration tracking
  • User-friendly interface
